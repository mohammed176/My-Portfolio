**Koshary** is Egypt's ultimate comfort food—a vibrant mix of rice, pasta, lentils, chickpeas, and crispy onions topped with zesty tomato sauce. It’s more than a meal; it’s a symbol of Egyptian street life, served with love at every corner.

**Fool** (also known as ful medames) is a staple of Egyptian breakfasts—slow-cooked fava beans seasoned with olive oil, cumin, garlic, and lemon juice. Served warm with fresh bread, it's humble yet deeply satisfying, fueling millions with its hearty simplicity and rich taste.
